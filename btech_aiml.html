The B.Tech AI & ML program trains students in core CS fundamentals plus applied machine learning, deep learning, data engineering, model deployment and ethical AI. Students learn to build, evaluate and scale intelligent systems used in real-world products.

Course Specifications

Duration 4 Years (8 Semesters)
Eligibility 10+2 with PCM / Entrance exam (JEE or institute-specific)
Intake 60 / 120 (depends on the institute)
Core Topics Linear Algebra, Probability, Data Structures, ML, Deep Learning, NLP, Computer Vision
Tools & Frameworks Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Scikit-learn, Docker, Kubernetes
Career Roles ML Engineer, Data Scientist, Research Engineer, MLOps Engineer

Syllabus (High-level)

  • Year 1: Mathematics for CS, Programming, Discrete Math, Physics
  • Year 2: Data Structures, Statistics & Probability, Database Systems
  • Year 3: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, NLP
  • Year 4: Big Data Engineering, MLOps, Major Project / Internship
