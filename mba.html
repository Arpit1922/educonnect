The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a prestigious postgraduate program designed to develop strong leadership, strategic thinking, and management skills. It equips students with the knowledge to manage businesses effectively in a dynamic global environment.

Course Specifications

Duration:
2 Years (4 Semesters)
Eligibility:
Graduation in any discipline with minimum 50% marks
Intake:
120 Students (per batch)
Fees:
As per institute norms
Specializations:
HR, Finance, Marketing, IT, Operations
Career Options:
Business Analyst, HR Manager, Marketing Executive

Syllabus Overview

  • Semester 1: Principles of Management, Business Communication, Accounting for Managers
  • Semester 2: Marketing Management, Human Resource Management, Business Research Methods
  • Semester 3: Financial Management, Operations Management, Strategic Management
  • Semester 4: Entrepreneurship Development, Major Project, Internship
