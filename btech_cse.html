B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) is a four-year undergraduate program focused on computing fundamentals, software engineering, systems design, and modern technologies like cloud, AI, and cybersecurity. It prepares students for technical roles in software development, research, and infrastructure.

Course Specifications

Duration:
4 Years (8 Semesters)
Eligibility:
10+2 with Mathematics / Relevant entrance exam
Intake:
Varies by institute (e.g., 120/180)
Fees:
As per institute / scholarship options available
Core Subjects:
Programming, Data Structures, OS, DBMS, CN, Algorithms
Career Options:
Software Engineer, SDE, DevOps, Researcher, System Designer

Syllabus (High-level)

  • Year 1: Engineering Mathematics, Basic Programming, Physics, Fundamentals of CS
  • Year 2: Data Structures & Algorithms, Computer Organization, Database Systems
  • Year 3: Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Software Engineering, Electives
  • Year 4: Advanced electives (AI, Cloud, Security), Major Project / Internship
