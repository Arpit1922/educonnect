M.Tech ‚Äî Master of Technology

Advanced postgraduate program focused on specialised engineering topics, research, and thesis work.
Duration: 2 Years
Eligibility: B.Tech / B.E + GATE / Institute Entrance

The M.Tech program offers in-depth knowledge and research opportunities in a chosen specialization (e.g., Computer Science, VLSI, AI, Cybersecurity). It emphasises rigorous coursework, independent research, and a dissertation/project that prepares students for industry leadership or doctoral studies.

Course Specifications

Duration
2 Years (4 Semesters)
Specializations
Computer Science, AI & ML, Cybersecurity, VLSI, Data Science, Networking
Eligibility
B.Tech / B.E in relevant discipline; GATE score or institute-specific entrance
Intake
Varies per department (e.g., 18 / 24 seats per specialization)
Focus
Advanced coursework, research methods, thesis/project, publication opportunities
Career Paths
Research Engineer, Senior Developer, R&D, PhD, Technical Lead

Syllabus (High-level)

  • Semester 1: Advanced core subjects, Research Methodology, Elective 1
  • Semester 2: Specialization courses, Elective 2, Mini project
  • Semester 3: Advanced seminars, thesis literature review, elective/project
  • Semester 4: Major Thesis / Dissertation, Publication & Industry internship
